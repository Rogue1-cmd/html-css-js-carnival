James Bondo shares a poem about one of his favourite things in life

"Thick Girls Were Made for Cuddling"

In curves and lines, a soft embrace,
Thick girls bring warmth to any space.
Their arms invite, their hearts so kind,
In their embrace, solace you'll find.

With every hug, a gentle hold,
Their presence comforts, never cold.
In their embrace, worries melt away,
Thick girls were made for cuddling, they say.

Their laughter fills the air with cheer,
In their arms, there's nothing to fear.
For in their curves, love's sweet nest,
Thick girls cuddle, and hearts find rest.

So let the world sing praises true,
For thick girls bring joy in all they do.
In their embrace, love unfurls,
For thick girls were made for cuddling, dear world.
